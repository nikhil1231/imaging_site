Advice for University

Interested in going to university? Wanting to study Computer Vision and Imaging? We have the right advice for you! Studying Computer Science at UCL will give you the skills and knowledge you need to succeed in the industry. During your time here, there will be many opportunities to meet and volunteer at the research group to further your knowledge. Furthermore, we have a wide selection of modules you can take to specialise into Computer Vision:

  • Machine Vision
  • Image Processing
  • Mathmatical Methods
  • Biomedical Imaging
  • Geometry of Images
